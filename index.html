Joy's Early Childhood Education Picture Books

For the Very Young

Black Ant goes on a journey to have tea with his friend. Along the way, he learns all about prepositions.

Red Ant goes on a journey to the shop, so that he will be ready for his friend to have tea. As he travels, he learns some new adjectives and adverbs.

For the Slightly Less Young

Oh no, Mikey has lost his knees. Join him as he journeys around the jungle and learns the value of working towards a goal.

Alphie has an exciting day ahead of him. He's going to escape from the mud monsters and run among the stars. As he does, he will learn the alphabet.

About Joy

Joy Penney is a well respected figure in the New Zealand Education Sector. She came to New Zealand from England and now lives in Wellington. She has always been interested in drawing and painting. Her passion for Early Childhood Education stems from the challenges involved in expressing seemingly simple concepts in a child friendly way.
