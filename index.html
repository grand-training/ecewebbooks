About Joy

Joy Penney is a well respected figure in the New Zealand Education Sector. She came to New Zealand from England and now lives in Wellington. She has always been interested in drawing and painting. Her passion for Early Childhood Education stems from the challenges involved in expressing seemingly simple concepts in a child friendly way. She is keen to encourage creativity in children of all ages.
